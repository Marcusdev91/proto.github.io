Debate and Public Speaking

Team participated in a school debate at Sekano Ntoane on the 14th of September 2024. The team made it to the semi-finals.

Activities like "Impromptu Speaking" and "Extemporaneous Speaking" was conducted in that students were given a topic and a short amount of time to prepare and deliver a speech. Tournaments were Organized with different debate formats (e.g., policy debate, Lincoln-Douglas debate) to give students a competitive experience.Students competed in multiple rounds, presenting their arguments and responding to their opponents. The Tournaments were very competitive they helped students hone their debate skills and gain experience.

Financial literacy

Learner participated in financial literacy. The learner got 3rd position. The competition consisted of two rounds—school and district. Students delivered a five-minute prepared speech on a prescribed financial literacy topics. Topics: Budgeting, savings, investments, consumer rights, and responsibilities.

Prizes: Top learners received fully funded bursaries worth approximately R150,000 to study at any public university in South Africa.

Knight Piesold Bokamoso Creative Essay Competition

In the Bokamoso Creative Essay Competition, more than 30 learners from Protea Glen participated. The coordinating team, led by Mr. Lekalakala, Mam Nyazeka, Mam Ntuli and Mam Lethoba, saw learner Esethu Khumalo rise to the top, securing 3rd place in a highly competitive field. Esethu was awarded R70,000 for the school and R5,000 for herself, cementing the school's reputation for fostering talent and creativity. These victories highlight Protea glen Secondary School No. 1s commitment to excellence in science on both individual and collective achievement.

Our history

The school was founded in 2009 and was opened on the 1st October 2009. The school at its onset Had 200 learners only who were in grade 08.These learners progressed with the school up until they Were in grade 12.this was our first matriculation class. The latter class produced 82, 2 % percent pass in the year 2013. The school is regarded as a great school where excellence is the order of the day. We pride ourselves In sports, our Netball team obtained Bronze in 2014 losing to Monde or High school in the Mr Price competition. We have 46 educators and 10 public service staff.
